Your questions answered

What is included in your platform?
Our platform includes Social Media Automation, CRM tools, AI-powered chatbots, real-time analytics, multi-platform post scheduling, and integration with tools like WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and more.
Is your CRM customizable?
Absolutely. Our CRM is flexible and fully customizable to match your business workflows, pipelines, and lead stages. You can even set automation rules to save time.
Do I need technical skills to use the platform?
Not at all! Our platform is designed for everyone — no coding or tech expertise needed. If you can use social media, you can use our tools.
Is there a free trial available?
Definitely. You can start with a 14-day free trial with full access to all features — no credit card required.
Can I schedule posts across multiple platforms?
Yes! You can schedule and publish posts across Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube, and more — all from one simple dashboard.

Does your platform support WhatsApp automation?
Yes, we support WhatsApp Business automation. You can send messages, set up chatbots, and automate replies based on customer triggers.
Can I integrate this with my existing tools?
Yes, we offer integrations with popular tools like Google Sheets, Mailchimp, HubSpot, Zapier, and more. Custom integrations are also available on request.
How do I get support if I face issues?
Our friendly support team is available via live chat, email, and WhatsApp. We also offer a detailed help center and onboarding assistance for new users.
