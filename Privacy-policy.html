Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when using our social media posting app (the "App ").

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information : When you link your social media accounts, we collect your profile ID, page ID, and access tokens required to post content.
  • Content Data : We store metadata such as captions, media URLs, and scheduled times for posts you create using the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To enable posting content to your connected social media platforms
  • To store your preferences and account linkage details
  • To send alerts when access tokens are expiring or actions are required

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • To the APIs of social media platforms you choose to connect (e.g., Meta/Facebook/Instagram)

4. Security

We use industry-standard security practices, including encryption and access control. However, no digital system can guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You can request data deletion at any time by contacting us at support@techalgo.online .

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. All updates will be posted here with the date of change.
